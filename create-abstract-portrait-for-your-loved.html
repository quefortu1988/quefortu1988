Create An Abstract Portrait For Your Loved One











About:

The process.

  1. You would provide me with a picture that special person you want this done for.
  2. I'll use that picture to create an abstract portrait. It would be cool, it would be stunning.
  3. I'll give you an SVG (scalable vector graphics) image. These images can be zoomed into any level and they never pixelate.
  4. Additionally, I'll also give you a JPEG image. It would come in handy if you want to share the picture quickly. JPEG would have maximum dimensions of 8192 x 8192 pixels, with 96 dpi.


Note:

  1. SVG opens in a browser. Right click your SVG file, choose "open with..", then select a browser you use.


What you can do with it?

  1. Later, you can use this SVG to print out a portrait for gifting. It would be quick and easy. You'll need a laser printer, a software like "Inkscape" or "GIMP", and the SVG I would give you. Print at your home and make an awesome portrait!
