Hand Embroider A Landscape Of Somewhere Special To You











About:

What am I offering?

I am offering a hand embroidered piece of art work, which is a wall hanging. I specialise in landscapes and buildings. I am known for creating pretty skies! Every stitch is hand done, hundreds or thousands of stitches create a piece of art you can keep forever in your home. 



Your piece could be of:

- Your favourite place in the whole world

- The place you got engaged

- A place that means a lot to you





On average a piece which measures approximately 8'' will typically take me around 20 hours, often more, depending on the landscape I am creating. I have hundreds and hundreds of threads in my collection to use to create your piece of art!
