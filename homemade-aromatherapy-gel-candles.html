Homemade Aromatherapy Gel Candles











About:

Aromatherapy Candles by Jasiah started at the beginning of 2020. We made a couple samples and passed them around to students, instructors, and community residents around the residential college we attend. Our feedback was positive and we have been selling our products ever since then. We make our candles with 100% essential oils with gel and have a wide range of scents. Our line of scents include lemon, cinnamon, peppermint, cherry sage, vanilla mint cream, citrus, coconut lavender, sandal sage, cinnamon clove, cinnamon burst, and peppermint paddie. Our buyers have said that our candles last very long and burn really well with a pleasant aroma. We also sell homemade infused wooden inspirational car fresheners. The best thing about our car fresheners are that they are durable and reusable. Not to mention, they are unique and utterly creative. As of right now we are operating off of our instagram and facebook pages where people contact us and make orders. Our instagram page is aromatherapycandlesbyjasiah and our facebook page is Jasiah Arts. Follow Us!!!
